MyFarm

MyFarm helps farmers around the world grow crops!

It's a free app that helps everyone find the best crops to grow. And it's 100% FREE. In addition to which crops to grow, you'll also get information on when and how to grow certain crops. Plus, MyFarm has an easy-to-use desktop app. You can save your crop data from the cloud, straight to your computer! MyFarm is the essential tool for any farmer.

Why we created this

Sometimes both farmers and gardeners have trouble finding crops to grow. We created MyFarm to fix this problem, by making open information about crops and plants easily accessible to everyone.
